For Namratha · Logistics accounts
Questions for your accounts

KGC — our reference account
Namratha onboarded KGC on driver fleet cards in one session — sub-wallets per vehicle, fuel only, mandatory receipt. This is the closest thing we have to a working proof of concept.
What made it work in that one call — and is it working in the field? That answer shapes the entire playbook.
Also ask: Have any logistics companies tried registering their Alaan card on Etisalat or DEWA for auto-debit — and did it work?
For Aaron · Corporate Services accounts
Questions for your accounts
Understand where spend is going
— What are your corp services accounts spending on every week that never shows up on Alaan?

— SZG — Jaufre wanted to pilot international payments. Has that started? What's blocking it?

SZG — our reference account
SZG is already on Alaan for petty cash and Zoho. Now wants to expand to international vendor payments — comparing Alaan's 1% FX markup against DIB's 3–4%.
What's blocking the expansion — product gaps or something on the customer side? That distinction matters a lot.
Also ask: Is there an account in your portfolio where you've basically given up trying to expand their spend? What makes them different from accounts that deepened?
To close the call — ask both
"If you could change one thing about how Alaan works that would make it easier for your customers to actually use it more — what would that be?"
Don't suggest an answer. Don't prompt with options. Just wait. The answer to this single question will shape more product decisions than anything else in this meeting.
